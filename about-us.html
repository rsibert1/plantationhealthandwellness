Brian S Hunt, D.C.

Chiropractic Physician

Expert in Chiropractise Treatment

Dr. Brian S Hunt is a graduate of the founding college of chiropractic, Palmer College of Chiropractic in Davenport, Iowa, where he earned his bachelor’s degree in science and a doctorate in chiropractic. While at Palmer he worked as a rehab intern, combining his fourteen years of experience as a clinical massage therapist with additional instruction at Palmer.

Since taking over the practice in 2021, Dr. Hunt has been named Best Chiropractor by Our City Best of Plantation for 2022, 2023 and 2024.

Dr. Hunt feels his life’s journey led him to become a chiropractor. While in college to be became a professional opera singer, a career he maintained for 17 years, he was involved in an auto collision. This experience introduced him to the benefits of chiropractic and laid the foundation for his chiropractic future. Later when studying to become a clinical massage therapist, his instructors constantly encouraged him to pursue becoming a chiropractor. As he continued to build a successful clinical massage practice in the New York City area, his patients echoed this sentiment, because Dr. Hunt always wanted to do everything possible to relieve a person’s pain.

Prior to joining Plantation Health and Wellness, he served as the Clinical Director at The Joint Chiropractic Weston, worked at Zitter Health Insights in market research, and for the North American Headquarters of Aston Martin, Jaguar, Land Rover.

He currently resides in Fort Lauderdale with his rescued, grey, tabby cat, Fiona. In his free time, he enjoys the performing arts, biking, cooking, and baking.

Marta Khaleel

Office Manager

Marta Khaleel was born in Texas. Her family moved to good ole Miami Beach when she was 7 months old, and she has been here ever since. Marta has three children, two girls and one boy. She also has a beautiful 3-year-old granddaughter who is the center of her life.

She was very blessed to be a homemaker for their elementary and middle school years and she was very active in all their school activities including the school’s PTA where she was the Treasurer and also the President. In 2007 she became a single parent and had to get into the workforce. Marta started as a receptionist at a gynecologist’s office and went back to school to become a nurse. She worked at Cleveland Clinic Florida in the Uro-Gynecology surgical department and after a few years there she decided to move on to an administrative position managing three MRI offices along with a Chiropractic/Orthopedic office. That is the moment she found her calling, resolving all administrative issues as well as helping patients start their journey into wellness through Chiropractic. Marta has been doing this for over 10 years and there is never a day that mirrors the previous day. She is continuously learning the importance of Chiropractic in our everyday life. Marta is happy to be part of the team at Plantation Health and Wellness where we strive to help everyone live life to their fullest potential.

Jennifer Ramirez

Wellness Coordinator

Jennifer Ramirez is the Wellness Coordinator for Plantation Health and Wellness. Originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina, she moved with her family to Boston, Massachusetts when she was two. After her family visited Orlando on vacation in 2000, they decided to move to Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

She has worked as a Patient Safety Assistant and Home Health Aide for the last six years. She has a passion for helping others and making a difference in their lives. As such, she is excited to learn and enhance her skills within our clinic.

Outside of the office, she currently finds a lot of her time occupied as the proud mother of three sons, ages 1, 4 and 6. In addition she enjoys being a party planner and a marketing and graphic designer for social media.
